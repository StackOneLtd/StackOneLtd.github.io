Busy Barrister

Working at the Manukau District Court, our client is a busy barrister whose days are filled with court appearances and client consultations.

Technology was an afterthought, often "managed" by well-meaning family and friends from younger generations. This ad-hoc approach led to a patchwork of solutions that didn't quite fit her professional needs.

Challenges

  • Missing Emails and Invites: Crucial emails and calendar invites were slipping through the cracks due to an outdated email service set up over a decade ago.
  • Lack of Sync Across Devices: Emails, calendars, and files weren't syncing because her old services didn't support modern technologies.
  • No Clear Support Expertise: Relying on family members without a holistic understanding of professional tech led to temporary fixes and "shadow IT" problems.
  • Unnecessary Costs: She was paying hundreds of dollars each month without knowing why. Each helper added new subscriptions, like 12 unnecessary Teams Essentials licenses, when a simple Microsoft 365 subscription would have sufficed at a fraction of the cost.
  • Security Concerns: Running two antivirus programs made her systems painfully slow. Without understanding security basics like multi-factor authentication (MFA), she was worried but unprotected.

The Solution

We started with a thorough review of her professional needs to tailor the right solution. We identified her pain points and implemented key technologies:

  • Microsoft 365: Streamlined her email, calendar, files, and Teams for seamless syncing across all devices.
  • Tenant-Wide Backup: Ensured all her data is securely backed up across Microsoft 365 services.
  • Intune Device Management: Provided secure and efficient management of all her devices.
